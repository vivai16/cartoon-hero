About Baymax

An Inflatable and huggable personal healthcare companion who is completely autonomous.

Baymax's characteristics

  1. He's very soft natured.
  2. He activates when he senses that someone is in pain.
  3. He walks slow
  4. He can scan with his eyes and check for symptoms
